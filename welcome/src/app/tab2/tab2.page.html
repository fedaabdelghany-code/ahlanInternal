Grand Egyptian Museum

Recently opened in early November, the world’s largest museum dedicated to a single civilization showcases Egypt’s legacy in iconic form. Lafarge supplied over 320,000 m³ of ultra-durable concrete, including white concrete, to ensure both beauty and longevity.

Iconic Tower

As Africa's tallest skyscraper, the Iconic Tower utilized Lafarge's Hydrocem Plus cement, known for its low heat of hydration and extended durability, ensuring structural integrity during its massive foundation pour

Metro Line 3

Enhancing Cairo's transportation, Lafarge contributed over 900,000 m³ of concrete to Metro Line 3, facilitating daily commutes for millions.
